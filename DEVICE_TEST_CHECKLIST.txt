Device Test Checklist (HearBuds)

1) Permissions
- Launch app on Android device.
- Verify microphone permission prompt appears.
- Accept permission and confirm UI unlocks.

2) DSP Pipeline
- Run calibration, then toggle DSP on.
- Confirm you can hear mic audio routed to earbuds.
- Toggle DSP off; audio should stop.
- If Bluetooth headset/earbuds are connected, verify the mic is used (not handset).

3) Calibration
- Start calibration in a quiet room.
- Verify tone levels change after “Heard it” / “Didn’t hear”.
- Complete calibration and confirm profile is marked Ready.
- Verify thresholds list appears in Advanced mode.

4) Noise Reduction
- Enable noise reduction, adjust strength.
- Capture room noise and verify clarity improves in steady noise.

5) Safety
- Enable limiter and safe mode in Advanced.
- Confirm no sharp spikes when loud input occurs.
- Verify safe mode caps overall loudness.

6) Profiles
- Save current profile and reload it after app restart.
- Switch between profiles and confirm settings apply.

7) Performance
- Verify callback ms remains stable.
- Confirm Input meter responds to sound.
